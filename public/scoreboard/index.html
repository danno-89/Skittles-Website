Skittles Scoreboard

Standard Game (5 Hands)

Each player plays 5 hands. The team with the highest total pinfall wins.
Killer (Lives)

Players start with lives and lose them if they don't hit the target. Last one standing wins.

Knockout Skittles

A high-stakes tournament format where players are eliminated each round.

Killer (Scores)

A variation on Killer focusing on achieving the highest score rather than eliminating opponents.

Round Robin

All teams play each other in a league format. Points are awarded for wins and draws.

Royal Table

A traditional game where players aim to knock down all the pins for the highest score.
