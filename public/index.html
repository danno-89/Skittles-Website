Sarnia Skittles Club Cup starts this week

The SSC Cup starts this week and has been designed as a Score Balanced competition to help level the playing field.

The competitions consists of a Group Stage with the top 2 teams from each group progressing to the knockout stage.

Following the AGM last season, the idea of a handicap was raised and, rather than introduce this into the leagues it was decided to run a seperate competition which would help analyse whether the system works, or where it would require tweaking.

For more information on the fixtures, group standings and handicaps, please see the SSC Cup page.
